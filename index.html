Your guide

This is a recruitment guide for junior rowers, who are interested in rowing and studying in the United States. At first glance, the process might seem bewildering, but it's more than possible to do on your own. This guide aims to break the process into 3 stages, which have some overlap.

  • 1. Prepare

    Before you get started, think about why you would like to study in the US. What will make it a good option for your future? Set clear goals.

    Get Ready!

  • 2. Make Contact

    Once you've decided it go ahead, it's time to contact teams and find out if they are a good fit for you. It's important to stick to your goals!

    Get Set!

  • 3. Commit

    After you choose a university, they'll guide you through the rest of the academic and financial aid application process.

    Go!
